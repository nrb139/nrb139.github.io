About Me

I'm Nolan Bennett, a photographer at heart and Penn State student studying Digital Multimedia Design (B.Des.) My journey with photography began as a way to document my travels and experiences, but it quickly evolved into a passionate hobby of capturing the world's beauty through my lens.

I've captured photos in some incredible locations, from the dramatic landscapes of Spain, to islands in the Caribbean, to the varied landscapes of Ecuador. Everywhere I've travelled has helped me develop a deep appreciation for diverse cultures and natural environments. These experiences have shaped my art and taught me to find extraordinary moments in everyday scenes.

My Approach

This site focuses on 3 areas of my photography: landscapes that showcase nature's grandeur, architecture that highlights human creativity, and wildlife that captures authentic moments in nature. Each genre requires different techniques and patience, but they all share a common goal— creating images that resonate with viewers and tell compelling visual stories.

Beyond Photography

When I'm not behind the camera, you'll find me hiking, camping, or working on other creative pursuits like calligraphy and crochet. I'm also passionate about mindfulness practices and enjoy exploring how photography can be a meditative practice - learning to be fully present in the moment while creating.

Let's Connect

I'm always interested in connecting with fellow photographers, potential clients, and anyone who appreciates visual storytelling. If you're interested in commissioning work, purchasing prints, or simply want to discuss photography techniques, feel free to reach out through my contact page.
